Sukha Surf and Adventures

My name
is Ruban.

I was ten years old the first time I stood on a board. The Shore Temple was behind me, the Bay of Bengal in front, and nobody had to teach me to love it. I just did.

I grew up in Mahabalipuram when it was quiet. Before the tourists, before the cafes. Just fishermen, temples, and the ocean. Surfing was mine. Something private. Something that made sense when nothing else did.

Then people started showing up on the beach. Curious. Nervous. Asking if they could try. And something happened the first time I helped someone stand up on a wave. I felt it more than when I ride myself.

That is why Sukha exists. Not because I planned a business. Because I could not stop.

We are not a surf school that happens to be in Mahabalipuram. We are Mahabalipuram. This beach is home. These waves are home. And when you come here, even for one morning, we mean it when we say you are family.

If you ever find yourself in Mahabalipuram, come find us. We will be on the beach.

Quick Answers

Questions?
We got you.

Do I need to know how to swim? +
No, you do not. We welcome non-swimmers to our beginner sessions. We stay in shallow water and Ruban is always right beside you. Just let us know when you book so we can plan accordingly.
What should I bring? +
Just swimwear, a towel, sunscreen, and a relaxed attitude. We provide surfboards, leashes, and rash guards. Water shoes are optional but useful as the sand gets hot in the afternoon.
When is the best time to surf here? +
October to March is peak season when the northeast swell brings consistent waves. April to September has smaller calmer surf that is perfect for beginners. We run sessions all year and always know what to expect each day.
How far from Chennai? +
About 50km south on the East Coast Road. Roughly 1 to 1.5 hours depending on traffic. One of the nicest coastal drives in Tamil Nadu too, so enjoy the ride.
Can children join a lesson? +
Yes, and honestly kids progress faster than adults. We recommend ages 8 and above. Ruban stays right beside them the whole time and our beginner boards are wide, stable, and safe.
Can I rent a board without a lesson? +
Absolutely. Fun boards from Rs 500 and softtops from Rs 700 for 90 minutes. Just WhatsApp us before you arrive so we have the right board ready.
Do you recommend places to eat nearby? +
That is one of our favourite things to do. We know every good food spot in Mahabalipuram, the places locals actually eat, the right spots for a group dinner on the beach. Just ask us when you arrive.
